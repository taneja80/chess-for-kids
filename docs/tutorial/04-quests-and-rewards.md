# 🏆 Page 4 — Quests, Badges, Avatars & the Hall of Fame

Playing chess is fun. **Earning rewards while you play** is even more fun. Here's everything you can collect, unlock, and show off in **Chess for Kids**.

---

## 🗺️ The Quest Map

Switch to the **🗺️ Quests** tab in the sidebar to leave the regular game and enter a world of bite‑sized chess puzzles.

Each quest is a real chess position with **one correct move** (or short sequence) that wins material or delivers checkmate. Merlin gives you **hints** if you're stuck, and you earn **gold** for every quest you solve.

### Example Quests
| # | Title | Skill Taught | Reward |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 🐉 **The Dragon Strikes!** | Mate in 1 | 50 gold |
| 2 | 🍴 **Fork Attack** | Knight fork | 50 gold |
| 3 | 📌 **Pin the Dragon** | Bishop pin | 75 gold |
| 4 | 🏰 **Back Rank Checkmate** | Back‑rank weakness | 75 gold |
| 5 | 🌪️ **The Windmill** | Combination tactics | 100 gold |

Quests are tagged by difficulty: **Squire**, **Knight**, **Bishop**. Solving them in order is the fastest way to level up your real‑game eye.

### Other training tabs
- **🏰 Endgame** — practice king + pawn, king + rook, and other endgame essentials
- **📚 Openings** — drill the most common opening moves so you never feel lost on move 1

---

## 🪙 Gold Coins

You earn gold from:

| Action | Gold |
|---|---|
| Capturing a Pawn | +1 |
| Capturing a Knight / Bishop | +3 |
| Capturing a Rook | +5 |
| Capturing a Queen | +9 |
| Winning a game | +25 |
| Solving a quest | 50–100 |
| Unlocking a badge | 25–100 |

Your current balance is always shown in the **Status Bar** at the top of the game. Spend it in the **🛒 Armoury Shop**.

---

## 🛒 The Armoury Shop

The Armoury is where your gold turns into **swagger**. There are two sections:

### 🎨 Board Themes
Customize how your chessboard looks. A few favorites:
- **Classic Wood** — the timeless oak‑and‑walnut look (free, default)
- **Neon Magic** — glowing cyan and magenta squares
- **Obsidian Volcanic** — dark molten reds and blacks
- **Emerald Forest** — deep greens with golden trim
- **Ice Citadel** — frosted blues and whites

### ⚔️ Knight Avatars
Pick the warrior who represents *you* in the Hall of Fame and the Wizard Coach panel:
- 🛡️ **Squire** — the humble starter (free, default)
- 🗡️ **Knight Errant** — wandering hero
- 🏹 **Royal Ranger** — sharpshooter of the realm
- 🔥 **Fire Archmage** — flame‑wielding magus
- 🌑 **Void Sorcerer** — master of dark arts
- 🐉 **Dragon Champion** — the legendary one (very expensive!)

Buying an avatar **automatically equips** it. You can switch back any time — once unlocked, it's yours forever.

---

## 🏅 Badges — The 12 Achievements

Badges are earned by hitting specific milestones. Open the **🏆 Hall of Fame** tab to see all 12, with progress bars for the ones you haven't unlocked yet.

A taste of what's in there:

| Badge | How to earn |
|---|---|
| 🥇 **First Victory** | Win your first game |
| 🐉 **Dragon Slayer** | Beat the **King** difficulty AI |
| 🏰 **Castle Builder** | Castle in 5 different games |
| 🧩 **Puzzle Master** | Solve every quest on the map |
| ⚡ **Spell Saver** | Win a game without using any Time Spells |
| 🎯 **Sharpshooter** | Capture the enemy Queen |
| 🛡️ **Defender** | Win without losing your own Queen |
| ♟ **Pawn Promoter** | Promote a pawn to a Queen |
| 🌐 **Friendly Rival** | Play your first multiplayer game |
| 👑 **Grand Master** | Reach 1000 gold |
| 🔥 **Hot Streak** | Win 3 games in a row |
| 🎓 **Apprentice** | Complete the tutorial |

Every badge you earn also pays out a gold bonus. Merlin celebrates with you each time.

---

## 🏆 The Hall of Fame

The **🏆 Hall of Fame** tab is your personal trophy room. It shows:

- Your **currently equipped knight avatar** front and center
- A **badge wall** — earned badges glow in full color; locked badges appear as faded outlines with a "How to earn" hint
- **Lifetime stats**: games played, games won, puzzles solved, gold earned, badges earned out of 12
- A **percentage completion** ring — your overall academy progress

It's the place to come back to when you want to feel proud of how far you've come — or to see what's left to chase.

---

## 🎯 Suggested Journey

If you're brand new, try this order:

1. 🎓 Complete the **Tutorial** → unlocks **Apprentice** badge
2. ⚔️ Play 3 games vs **Squire** AI → likely earns **First Victory** and **Hot Streak**
3. 🗺️ Solve quests 1–3 on the **Quest Map** → puzzle gold + maybe **Sharpshooter**
4. 🛒 Buy your first new **Knight Avatar** with your gold
5. 🌐 Invite a friend for a **multiplayer match** → unlocks **Friendly Rival**
6. ⚔️ Crank the AI up to **Knight**, then **Bishop**, then **King**
7. 🐉 Beat the King AI → **Dragon Slayer** badge!
8. 🏆 Open the Hall of Fame and admire your handiwork
