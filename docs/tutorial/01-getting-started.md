# 📖 Page 1 — Getting Started

Welcome to **Chess for Kids**! This is the magical chess academy where young knights learn the ancient game of strategy from Merlin himself. Before you draw your sword, let's get you to the castle gates.

---

## 🌐 Where to Play

You can play **Chess for Kids** right in your web browser. No download, no installer — just open the link:

👉 **<https://chess-for-kids-one.vercel.app>**

It works on:
- 💻 **Desktop / Laptop** — Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari
- 📱 **Phone or Tablet** — tap the squares to move pieces
- 🌍 **Any modern browser** with JavaScript enabled

> **Tip for parents:** the game runs entirely in the browser. The chess engine (Stockfish) runs locally — no data leaves the device — *except* when you choose to play a multiplayer game with a friend, which uses an online room code.

---

## 🏰 The Home Screen

When you open the app, you'll see the **Medieval Chess Academy** landing page. Floating chess pieces dance in the background, and two big buttons greet you:

| Button | What it does |
|---|---|
| ⚔️ **Play Now!** | Jumps straight into a single‑player game against the AI |
| 🎓 **Learn First** | Opens the **Tutorial** — meet every piece one at a time |

Below the buttons, you'll see:
- A **showcase of all 6 chess pieces** (King, Queen, Rook, Bishop, Knight, Pawn) with their crowns and colors
- A **"Why Kids Love It"** grid highlighting the Wizard Coach, difficulty levels, badges, gold coins, takebacks, and the tutorial
- A **"Your Quest Begins"** roadmap (Tutorial → Battle AI → Earn Badges)

---

## 🎓 First Stop: The Tutorial

If you've **never played chess before**, click **🎓 Learn First**. The Tutorial walks you through:

1. **♟ The Pawn** — your foot soldier
2. **♜ The Rook** — the castle tower
3. **♞ The Knight** — the leaping warrior
4. **♝ The Bishop** — the diagonal sage
5. **♛ The Queen** — the most powerful piece
6. **♚ The King** — the one you must protect

For each piece, **Merlin** explains how it moves, highlights the squares it can reach, and lets you click to practice. There's no opponent in the Tutorial — it's a calm, friendly playground.

When you're ready, head back to the home page and click **⚔️ Play Now!**.

---

## 🎮 Your First Game

Clicking **Play Now!** takes you to the **Battle Arena** (`/game`). You'll see:

- A big **8×8 chess board** in the center
- A **Wizard Coach panel** with Merlin's portrait and a speech bubble
- A **Sidebar** on the right with tabs across the top:
  - **⚔️ Arena** — the main game
  - **🗺️ Quests** — puzzle challenges
  - **🏰 Endgame** — endgame training
  - **📚 Openings** — opening practice
  - **🛒 Armoury** — buy themes & knight avatars
  - **🏆 Hall of Fame** — your trophy room
- A **status bar** showing your gold coins, AI difficulty, and time spells

**You play White** by default (the pieces at the bottom). Click one of your pieces — Merlin will highlight every legal square it can move to. Click a highlighted square to move. Done!

The **AI plays Black** and will respond automatically after a short "thinking" pause.

---

## 🔧 Quick Controls

| Action | How |
|---|---|
| **Select a piece** | Click (or tap) it |
| **Move** | Click a highlighted destination square |
| **Cancel selection** | Click the same piece again, or any empty square |
| **Undo last move** | Click **↩️ Takeback** in the controls |
| **New game** | Click **🆕 New Game** |
| **Change AI level** | Pick Squire / Knight / Bishop / King in the sidebar |
| **Toggle Territory Map** | Click 🔮 in the sidebar |
